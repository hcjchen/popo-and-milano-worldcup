# 🐱⚽ Popo & Milano's World Cup Quiz Shootout

A cute, single-page football game starring **Popo** (orange-and-white cat striker) and **Milano** (tuxedo-cat goalkeeper). Answer 5 daily World Cup trivia questions — every correct answer earns Popo a penalty kick to beat Milano!

## ▶️ Play now

**https://hcjchen.github.io/popo-and-milano-worldcup/**

Works on computers and phones. The same 5 questions appear for everyone each day, so you can compare scores with friends.

## How to play

1. Type your **name** (and an optional **squad number**, e.g. James #10).
2. Answer each question — **take your time, questions are untimed**. Each day's set is **Easy · Easy · Medium · Medium · Hard**, with no two questions of the same type. Points: Easy 100 · Medium 150 · Hard 250.
3. After you answer, read the fun fact, then click the button: a correct answer shows **"Take the penalty kick! ⚽"**, a wrong one shows **"Next question →"**.
4. In the penalty round, aim with your mouse/finger, hold to charge power, and release to shoot past Milano (who gets tougher on the later questions).
5. **Streak bonus:** answering correctly in a row gives more shooting time — 5s, then 7, 8, 9, 10s across a perfect 5/5 run.
6. After 5 questions, see your score and grade. Tap **Share** to copy your result + game link, or **📊 WC Standings** to view the live FIFA group tables.

**Score** = quiz points + 50 per goal.

## Features

- 170+ verified questions — World Cup history, hosts, winners, legends, team nicknames, 2026 facts, plus general soccer basics
- Daily challenge: the same 5 questions for everyone each day, always a varied mix of topics
- Difficulty tiers (Easy / Medium / Hard) with higher rewards for harder questions
- Click-to-continue after each question (untimed) with a fun fact every time
- Streak bonus: consecutive correct answers earn longer penalty rounds
- Penalty shootout with a charging power meter, Popo's kick animation, and a diving keeper that ramps up in difficulty
- Live FIFA World Cup standings button on the results screen
- Name + optional squad number, remembered on your device
- Best score saved on your device
- Mobile + desktop friendly, no installs
